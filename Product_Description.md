# Jesty

*The Chrome extension that roasts your open tabs*

## What is Jesty?

Jesty is a playful Chrome extension that reads your open tabs and responds with a single, personalized roast. It reacts in seven different moods and tones, mixing humor with mild confrontation. Cute, funny, and just uncomfortable enough to make you pause and reconsider that 47th tab.

## The Problem

Opening a new browser tab is a subtle but real increase in cognitive load. What starts as a simple action quickly turns into dozens of open tabs that are easy to forget and hard to manage. This silent accumulation reduces focus and hurts productivity. Most tab management tools respond with metrics and dashboards. Jesty responds with personality.

## How It Works

Every time you open a new tab, Jesty appears. It reads your tab titles, picks a mood, and delivers a roast tailored to your current browser chaos. The character's expression changes to match the tone of the message. Hit refresh for a new roast. That's it.

## Example Roasts

*"Gmail has more unread emails than actual friends. Face the chaos."*

*"You opened a PDF of your own resume? A bit much, don't you think? Close it."*

*"Portfolio projects and a stand-up comedy video. Laughing at your future? Prioritize real work."*

## Free Tier

The free tier is a complete experience: dynamic roasts generated by AI, seven different moods and tones, character expressions that change with each message, and initial character customization (style and color) at install. Free forever.

Privacy note: Tab titles are processed to generate roasts but never stored.

## Premium Tier ($5 one-time)

Premium unlocks Jesty as a productivity companion. Continue the conversation via a chat sidebar, always focused on helping you reduce cognitive debt and regain focus. Jesty gives you small, achievable tasks ("Close 5 tabs you haven't looked at in an hour"). Complete tasks to unlock cosmetic changes and watch Jesty evolve over time, Tamagotchi-style. The more focused you become, the more your companion grows.

## Progression System

Expression changes happen in real-time based on the roast mood (free tier). Cosmetic unlocks (accessories, backgrounds) come from completing tasks (premium). Evolution stages transform Jesty's form at major milestones, creating screenshot-worthy moments that feed back into virality.

## Built for Sharing

A funny roast about someone's specific tabs is screenshot-worthy. People will share these on social media without being asked. The personalized nature of each roast ("Cryptocurrency and a resume open? Hedging your bets on employment?") makes every share feel unique.

## Technical Notes

Roasts are generated dynamically via LLM. User progression and preferences are stored locally using Chrome's storage API. Premium status is validated via Stripe checkout with email-based activation. No heavy backend infrastructure required.

## Positioning

Jesty is entertainment-first with a productivity wrapper. It's not trying to be a serious tool that happens to be funny. It is the joke, which gives it permission to be weird. The $5 impulse price point converts users who smiled at their first 10 roasts.
