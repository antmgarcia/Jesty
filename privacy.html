Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What Jesty accesses

Jesty reads your open browser tab titles and URLs to generate roasts. This data is:

  • Processed locally on your device
  • Sent to OpenAI's API only when generating a roast or chat response
  • Never stored on any server we control
  • Never shared with third parties

What Jesty stores

All data is stored locally in your browser via Chrome's storage API:

  • Your roast history (last 100 roasts)
  • Usage stats (roast count, streak, browsing patterns)
  • Conversation history (last 50 chats)
  • Your color and accessory preferences

This data never leaves your device except when sent to OpenAI for generating responses.

OpenAI API

When generating roasts or chat responses, your tab titles and URLs are sent to OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini model. OpenAI's data usage policy applies to these API calls. We send only the minimum data needed for the feature to work.

No tracking

Jesty does not use analytics, cookies, or any tracking services. There are no ads. Your browsing data stays between you and your browser.

Data deletion

Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data. You can also clear data manually through Chrome's extension settings.
